The Angler’s House, a beautiful home on the upper Campbell River. Fisherman love our property as we have one of the best stretches to fish a few steps from the door. Watch anglers catch salmon, view bears foraging and eagles soar in their natural environment!

The Space

Our location is incredible for those who love rivers, fishing and wildlife and the outdoors in general.

Guest Access

You can access all city amenities within a 5 minute drive. Incredible hiking, biking trails and access to Elk Falls provincial park, the new suspension bridge and so much more within walking distance.

Interaction with Guests

We guide many guests flyfishing in the area and offer float trips down the Campbell River daily! We're always available if info is needed, but are not at the property unless needed.

The Neighbourhood

Our location suits the whole family with many great outdoor activities and all city amenities just minutes away. Catch wild Salmon in the back yard or go hike the Canyon View Trail a few minutes walk upstream! Watch or photograph Black Bears and Bald Eagles on the other side of the river from the comforts of home.

Getting around

Its a 5 minute walk to public transit if needed. Within an hours drive there are so many lakes, rivers and ocean front trails and beaches to access including Strathcona Park!
